SWAKSHTA AWARENESS

Devbhoomi Public School, Haldiwani, Uttarakhand

The Dastak Foundation has begun its journey towards its mission of empowering women and children through different means. Keeping its mission and vision in view, the Foundation conducted its first awareness programme on cleanliness among a group of school children in Haldwani, Uttarakhand on December 3, 2016.

A team of the Dastak Foundation, including its Presdient Ranjeeta Jha and General Secretary Ajay Jha made this maiden and appreciable effort and organised a cleanliness camp among a group of 80 school students belonging to Devbhumi Academy, Manpur West, Haldwani. With the support of school administration (Founder- Bhagirathi Bist & Principal- Puja Bist) and Block President (Bhola Dutt Bhatt) , the Dastak Foundation conducted a quiz competition on cleanliness and hygiene and a painting competition on the theme of ‘Swacchta Abhiyan’. Children also expressed their view on the cleanliness by delivering extempore speeches. They made the programme interesting by exhibiting their talent of singing. At the end of the programme a token of appreciation was presented to all children in the form of small gifts.

Though, students were from primary level (class 1 to class 5), but their keen participation into the programme was highly appreciable. The Dastak Foundation’s message on the ‘Swacchta Abhiyan’ was rightly received by the small children. In fact, the Foundation takes this stepping stone as a mile stone in its journey towards its mission and plans many more such programmes in coming days and ready to explore new avenues for disseminating its vision of empowering women and children in order to bring some substantial changes at grass-root level.

PAALI AWARENESS

Jaitpur, Pali, Rajasthan

Dastak foundation had organised a one day camp in Jaitpur, Pali (Rajasthan) where foundation voulentier with the help of lady doctors and local support tried to aware the poor village women to use sanitary napkins. Every year in India specially unprivileged rural women suffer many diseases due to use unhygienic clothes during their monthly mansuration. Foundation tried to make them aware to use low cost napkins with the help of gynecologist. In their counseling doctors advised them it is not only necessary to use napkins to avoid disease but it is their fundamental right and better future of their family. In the camp foundation had distributed hundreds of sanitry napkins to rural womens of different age groups. Along with this our lady doctors distributed medicines to women's on water born diseases.
Our Foundation vowed to spread this mission to different part of rural India. We are going to launch massive campaign in near future to different parts of country..Our Foundation President Ranjeeta jha had said on this occasion that if a women of the family is healthy then she can teach and make our healthy and prosperous.
In the end Dastak Foundation wants to thank all our volunteers ,our Gynecologist and local supporters to make our mission successful.
